Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, and more.

Are Bloomify flowers realistic looking?

Yes! Our artificial flowers are crafted using advanced 3D/4D printing technology and high-quality materials to create incredibly lifelike blooms. Each petal and leaf is designed to mimic nature's beauty while lasting much longer than real flowers.

What materials are used in your products?

We use eco-friendly materials including recycled polyester, silk-like fabrics, and sustainable plastics. All our materials are non-toxic and safe for home use. Our 4D collection features special materials that respond to light and temperature changes for an even more realistic effect.

What are your shipping options and costs?

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days - $5.99
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days - $12.99
  • Free Shipping: On all orders over $75 (standard shipping)

International shipping rates vary by destination.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on your location and shipping method selected:

  • USA: 2-5 business days
  • Canada: 5-10 business days
  • Europe: 7-14 business days
  • Other International: 10-21 business days

You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused products in their original packaging. To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our customer service team
  2. Receive your return authorization
  3. Ship the item back to us

Once received, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Do you offer exchanges?

Yes! We're happy to exchange items within 30 days of purchase. Simply contact our customer service team to arrange an exchange. You may be responsible for additional shipping costs if the exchange is for an item of higher value.

How do I clean and maintain my artificial flowers?

Our flowers require minimal maintenance:

  • Dust regularly with a soft brush or use compressed air
  • For deeper cleaning, use a slightly damp cloth
  • Avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading
  • Keep away from heat sources
  • For our 4D collection, avoid excessive moisture

How long do Bloomify flowers typically last?

With proper care, our artificial flowers can maintain their beauty for years. The average lifespan is 3-5 years for our standard collection and 5-7 years for our premium 4D collection. Many customers report their blooms looking as good as new even after a decade with occasional dusting.
