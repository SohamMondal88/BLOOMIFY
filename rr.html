Our commitment to your satisfaction with our eco-friendly floral products

Sustainable Returns

At Bloomify, we encourage responsible consumption. Before returning an item, please consider repurposing or donating your artificial flowers. If you do need to return, we'll ensure the process is as eco-friendly as possible.

Returns Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your Bloomify purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a refund or exchange.

Important Exceptions

The following items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective:

  • Custom or made-to-order floral arrangements
  • Final sale items marked "non-returnable"
  • Opened or used floral accessories (vases, ribbons, etc.)

Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

1
Contact our customer service at returns@bloomify.com within 30 days of delivery with your order number and reason for return.
2
Wait for approval. We'll review your request and send you return instructions within 2 business days.
3
Package your return using the original packaging if possible. If not, please use eco-friendly materials.
4
Ship your return to the address we provide. You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error.

Refund Policy

Once your return is received and inspected (typically within 5 business days), we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

  • Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 10 business days.
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
  • Exchanges will be processed once we receive your return. You will be responsible for any price difference and additional shipping costs.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund after 15 business days, please contact your bank or credit card company first as processing times vary. If you still haven't received your refund, contact us at support@bloomify.com.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@bloomify.com with photos of the damage and your order number.

We will arrange for a replacement or refund and provide a prepaid return label if needed. Our goal is to resolve such issues within 3 business days.

Eco-Friendly Return Shipping

When returning items, we encourage you to:

  • Reuse the original packaging if possible
  • Use recycled or biodegradable packing materials
  • Choose carbon-neutral shipping options if available

For every eco-friendly return we receive, Bloomify plants a tree through our partnership with One Tree Planted.
