Sizing Tips & Recommendations

Measuring Your Space

Use a tape measure to determine the available space. Leave at least 6 inches of clearance around arrangements for a balanced look.

Table Centerpieces

For dining tables, choose arrangements that are no more than 1/3 the width of the table to allow room for place settings.

Entryway Displays

Large floor arrangements should be proportional to your ceiling height - taller ceilings can accommodate taller arrangements.

Wall Decor

Wreaths and wall arrangements should be about 1/4 to 1/3 the size of the wall or door they're decorating.
