Our Blooming Story

Creating sustainable beauty that lasts, one artificial flower at a time

How We Started

Bloomify was founded in 2018 by environmentalist and floral designer Emma Richardson. After years of seeing the waste generated by the floral industry, Emma set out to create beautiful, eco-friendly alternatives that wouldn't harm the planet.

What began as a small workshop crafting flowers from recycled materials has blossomed into a thriving business with a mission to revolutionize home decor with sustainable solutions.
