Product Description

Our Elegant Rose Bouquet is crafted with the finest materials to replicate the beauty of real roses without the maintenance. Each stem features realistic petals with delicate veining and natural color variations for an authentic look.

The bouquet comes arranged with lush greenery and is ready to display in your favorite vase. Perfect for:

  • Wedding centerpieces and bouquets
  • Home decor - living room, dining table, bedroom
  • Office decor that lasts
  • Gifts for anniversaries, birthdays, or special occasions

Care Instructions

To keep your artificial roses looking their best:

  1. Dust lightly with a soft brush or cloth
  2. Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent fading
  3. Store in a cool, dry place when not in use
