Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

At Bloomify, we're committed to sustainability. All our artificial flowers are shipped in 100% recyclable and biodegradable packaging materials to minimize our environmental impact.

Domestic Shipping

We currently ship to all 50 U.S. states with the following options:

  • Standard Shipping (3-5 business days): $5.99 or FREE on orders over $50
  • Expedited Shipping (2-3 business days): $12.99
  • Overnight Shipping (1 business day): $24.99 (order must be placed by 12pm PST)

Delivery times are estimates and not guaranteed, as delays may occur due to carrier issues or unforeseen circumstances.

International Shipping

We currently offer international shipping to select countries. International orders typically take 7-21 business days to arrive, depending on the destination and customs processing.

International customers are responsible for any customs fees, taxes, or duties that may apply to their order. These charges are determined by the destination country and are not included in the shipping cost.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your order using the provided tracking number on the carrier's website.

If you're having trouble tracking your order or it hasn't arrived within the estimated timeframe, please contact us at shipping@bloomify.com.

Shipping Restrictions

Some of our larger floral arrangements may have shipping restrictions to certain locations due to size or carrier limitations. If this applies to your order, we will contact you with alternative options.

Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged or you suspect it is lost, please contact us immediately at support@bloomify.com with your order number and photos of the damaged items/packaging (if applicable).

We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and arrange for a replacement or refund as appropriate.
