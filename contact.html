Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are your flowers made from?

+

Our flowers are crafted from a variety of eco-friendly materials including recycled fabrics, biodegradable plastics, and sustainable fibers. Each product listing details the specific materials used.

How do you ensure your products are environmentally friendly?

+

We carefully vet all materials for sustainability, use non-toxic dyes, minimize waste in production, and offset our carbon emissions. Our packaging is also 100% recyclable or compostable.

Can I return or exchange my purchase?

+

Yes! We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items. Due to the sustainable nature of our business, we ask that you cover return shipping costs unless the item is defective.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk pricing?

+

We do offer wholesale pricing for businesses and events. Please contact our sales team at wholesale@bloomify.com for more information and pricing details.

How do I care for my artificial flowers?

+

Our flowers require minimal care - just occasional dusting with a soft cloth or gentle blow dryer on cool setting. Avoid direct sunlight for extended periods to prevent fading.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship worldwide! International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. All international orders include carbon offset shipping.
